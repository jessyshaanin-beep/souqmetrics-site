Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

SouqMetrics ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the data of merchants who use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

When you connect your store or advertising accounts to SouqMetrics, we may access the following types of data:

Commerce & Order Data

  • Order identifiers
  • Order dates and totals
  • Currency and country information
  • Order source and sales channel

This data is used solely to provide sales analytics and reporting.

Marketing & Attribution Data

  • UTM parameters (e.g. source, medium, campaign)
  • Advertising click identifiers (e.g. Meta, Google, TikTok)
  • Referrer information

Attribution data is collected to help merchants understand which marketing channels contribute to revenue. This data is attached to orders as metadata and is not used for advertising or tracking outside the merchantâ€™s account.

Advertising Platform Data

When you connect advertising platforms such as Meta (Facebook and Instagram), Google Ads, or TikTok Ads, we may access:

  • Advertising account identifiers
  • Campaign and ad performance metrics
  • Advertising spend and conversion statistics

We do not collect personal social media profile data, private messages, or passwords.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use collected data solely to:

  • Display sales and revenue analytics
  • Calculate marketing performance metrics such as ROAS and CPA
  • Attribute revenue to marketing channels
  • Provide insights and reporting to merchants

SouqMetrics does not sell data, share data with third parties for advertising purposes, or use data for profiling consumers.

3. Data Storage & Security

Data is stored securely using Supabase infrastructure. Access is restricted to authorized users associated with the connected business. Industry-standard security practices are applied to protect stored data.

4. Third-Party Services

SouqMetrics integrates with third-party platforms such as Shopify, WooCommerce, Meta Platforms, Google, and TikTok. Data access and usage comply with the respective platform terms and policies.

5. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide analytics and reporting features. Merchants may request deletion of their data at any time.
