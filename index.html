SouqMetrics

SouqMetrics is a marketing analytics and attribution platform that helps e-commerce businesses understand performance across ads and sales channels.

Supported integrations

  • TikTok Ads – read ad performance, spend, and conversions
  • Meta Ads – analyze paid social revenue and ROAS
  • Google Ads – track paid search performance
  • Shopify – sync orders and revenue for attribution

How the integration works

Users sign in to SouqMetrics and connect their ad accounts and store. SouqMetrics securely reads performance data via official APIs and attributes ad performance to real sales inside the dashboard.

Authentication & sandbox usage

Access to the dashboard requires authentication. For platform reviews, SouqMetrics supports sandbox / test accounts. The review video demonstrates connecting a sandbox account and viewing data inside the app.
